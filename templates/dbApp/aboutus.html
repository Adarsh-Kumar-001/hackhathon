Our Story

Founded in 2024, Daily Basis was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves access to fresh, quality groceries without the hassle of traditional shopping. We started as a small local initiative and have grown into a trusted community partner.

Our commitment to sustainability, quality, and customer satisfaction drives everything we do. We work directly with local farmers and trusted suppliers to ensure that every product meets our high standards.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of families, helping them save time while enjoying the finest groceries delivered fresh to their homes.
